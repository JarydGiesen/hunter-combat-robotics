Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about joining our league

We offer multiple weight classes including 1lb (Antweight), 3lb (Beetleweight), 12lb (Hobbyweight), 30lb (Featherweight), and 220lb (Heavyweight). Each class has specific rules and requirements designed for fair competition.

All robots must pass a thorough safety inspection before competing. This includes proper containment systems, safe electrical practices, weapon limits, and emergency shutdown mechanisms. We provide detailed safety guidelines in our rulebook.

Absolutely! We have extensive resources for beginners including build guides, recommended parts lists, mentor programs, and beginner-friendly workshops. Our Discord community is also incredibly welcoming and helpful for new builders.

Entry fees vary by weight class and event type, typically ranging from $25-$150 per robot. Prize pools are substantial, with major championships offering $10,000+ in prizes. We also offer sponsorship opportunities for dedicated competitors.

We host events quarterly across the region, with venues in major cities like Dallas, Austin, and Houston. Events are typically weekend-long tournaments held at convention centers or specialized arenas. Check our events calendar for the latest schedule.
